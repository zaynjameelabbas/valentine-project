SURGERY PREP

Strict NPO Protocol

Do not eat or drink anything after midnight the night before your surgery. Failure to comply will result in immediate cancellation of your procedure.

Pre-Operative Timeline

  • 7 Days Before Stop taking NSAIDs (Advil, Aleve) and blood thinners as directed by your clinical team.
  • Night Before Shower using the provided Chlorhexidine scrub. Do not apply lotions.
  • Morning Of Arrive at the surgical center exactly 2 hours prior to your scheduled block time. Bring ID.
