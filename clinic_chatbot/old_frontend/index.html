Clinical Frameworks

Procedures engineered for phased, safe return to high-impact sport.

ACDF

ACDF (anterior cervical discectomy and fusion) surgery is a procedure that relieves pain caused by pinched nerves in your neck. A surgeon will remove the disk between your affected bones, and then weld (fuse) them together.

Lumbar Fusion

Spinal fusion is surgery to connect two or more bones in any part of the spine. Connecting the bones prevents movement between them. Preventing movement helps prevent pain.

Microdiscectomy

Typically performed for a herniated disc, a microdiscectomy, also called a microdecompression, relieves the pressure on a spinal nerve root by removing the material causing the pain.
